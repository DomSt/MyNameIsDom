Auto Width Cells

cell, cell-sm, cell-md, cell-lg

Cells without a specified width are auto-sizing.

          
    <div class="group">
      <div class="cell-3"> CONTENT </div>
      <div class="cell"> CONTENT </div>
      </div class="cell-4"> CONTENT </div>
    </div>

Nesting

group, cell, cell--collapsed

Grids can be nested inside other grids. But beware of inception ;)

          
    <div class="group">
      <div class="cell-6">
        <div class="group">
          <div class="cell-3"> CONTENT </div>
          <div class="cell-9"> CONTENT </div>
        </div>
      </div>
      <div class="cell-6">
        <div class="group">
          <div class="cell-6"> CONTENT </div>
          <div class="cell-6"> CONTENT </div>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>

Offsets

cell--offset-1, cell-sm--offset-2, cell-md--offset-3, cell-lg--offset-4, ...

Cells can be reposition in single-column steps with the --offset modificator.

          
    <div class="group">
      <div class="cell-6 cell--offset-6"> CONTENT </div>
    </div>
